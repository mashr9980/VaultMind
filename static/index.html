Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VaultMind

How does VaultMind work completely offline?

VaultMind installs directly on your servers or workstations and includes all necessary AI models locally. Once installed, it requires no internet connection to process documents or answer questions. Your data never travels outside your infrastructure.

What file formats does VaultMind support?

VaultMind supports PDF, Word documents (DOCX), Excel spreadsheets (XLSX), text files (TXT), and more. We're continuously adding support for additional formats based on customer needs.

How accurate are the AI responses?

VaultMind uses state-of-the-art language models specifically optimized for document analysis. The system provides confidence scores with each answer and cites specific document sources, ensuring high accuracy and transparency.

What are the system requirements?

VaultMind runs on Windows, macOS, or Linux systems with at least 16GB RAM and 50GB storage. For enterprise deployments, we recommend dedicated servers. GPU acceleration is optional but improves performance.

Can I integrate VaultMind with existing systems?

Yes! VaultMind provides REST APIs for integration with your existing workflows. Enterprise plans include custom integration support and SSO compatibility with Active Directory and other authentication systems.

What happens during the free trial?

During your 30-day free trial, you get full access to VaultMind's features. Our team will help you install the system, upload your documents, and train your users. No credit card required to start.

How do updates and maintenance work?

VaultMind includes automatic update mechanisms that work offline. Updates are delivered as secure packages that you can review before applying. Our support team assists with maintenance and optimization.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive support including installation assistance, user training, and ongoing technical support. Enterprise customers get dedicated support managers and 24/7 assistance for critical issues.
